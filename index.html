About Me

I, Mike Lee Thomas, often find myself reflecting on the colorful journey that has shaped my life. Growing up in a small town, I first stumbled upon my passion for art in my grandmother’s attic, where I would lose myself in the dust-covered canvases and brushes that seemed to hold forgotten stories. For years, I drifted through various odd jobs, my spirit restless and my dreams on hold, yet I always returned to sketching the world around me. It wasn’t until I stepped away from my routine office job that I fully embraced my artistic calling. With renewed energy, I began to pour decades of experience and emotion onto the canvas. To my astonishment, my vibrant, expressive works caught the attention of the art community, and soon my paintings adorned gallery walls, each piece a chapter of my story. My journey is a reminder that it’s never too late to pursue your passions, showing that the heart of an artist only grows deeper and more vibrant with time.
